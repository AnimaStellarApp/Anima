Payment system

Stellar Wallet connection + interior payment system. Immediate low-cost payments are possible all over the world. Users can directly obtain, transfer, buy NFTs and display them.

QR Code scanner

The QR code scanner is a key feature that allows users to scan an NFT to obtain it and connect directly the user to the related informations.

NFTs with ANIMA

By opening a profile in ANIMA institutions from different sectors can create a gallery for their business where users can find informations and services.

We believe that the new horizons of NFTs are in the utilities and benefits that people can access with them.

Our mission is to deliver a service able to give real uses to NFTs in multiple aspects of our life. ANIMA provides a platform through which users can open a profile to obtain, buy, exchange NFTs and directly use them.
